The writer creates an atmosphere of quiet permanence blended with subtle mystery, using carefully chosen words, phrases and language techniques to immerse the reader in the library’s unique character. Lexical choices establish the space as aged yet carefully maintained: “dust drifted through the pale light” and “collected on the shelves and in the grooves of the floorboards” evoke stillness and timelessness, while sensory details like “dry and faintly bitter” (describing paper and ink) and “cracked spines” alongside “softened by use” balance wear with warmth, suggesting the library holds living history rather than decay. Personification gives the building agency- it is “preserved, carefully held in place, as through change was something that happened reluctantly here”- framing it as a guardian of its contents. Layered imagery, from visual details of faded photographs and light play to spatial descriptions of shelves that “stretched deeper”, creates a tangible environment, while phrases like “ curved out of sight” and “loosing your  sense of direction” introduce gentle unease, hinting the space can alter a visitor’s focus and purpose. A metaphorical undercurrent also runs through the text: the idea that the library can make “the reason you had come no longer matter” suggests it has the power to shift perspective, adding depth to its mysterious quality.

Sentence structure is used deliberately to control rhythm and emphasis, further shaping the atmosphere. Short, declarative statements like “it was everywhere” and “The space felt preserved” anchor key ideas, making the library’s defining traits clear and memorable. In contrast, longer complex sentences such as “Encyclopaedias paperbacks softened by use. Old magazines were stacked in uneven piles, their faded photographs still visible at the edges” build detail gradually, mirroring how Nia would take in her surroundings step by step. The shift from descriptive to speculative in “The shelves stretched deeper into the building, forming long aisles that curved out of sight. It was easy to imagine loosing your sense of direction…” slows the pace and encourages the reader  to share in Nia’s growing sense of immersion. In combination, all these elements create an atmosphere that is both comforting and unsettling- one that feels rooted in the past yet capable of of shaping the present moment for those who enter.
