The writer structures the text by gradually shifting focus, which helps to engage the reader and build tension.

At the start, the focus is on the contrast between the busy street and the sudden silence inside the building. When the “noise from the street disappeared completely”, it signals that the library is separate from the outside world, immediately creating intrigue.

As the text develops, the focus moves deeper into the building with detailed descriptions of shelves, dust and aisles. This slows the pace and mirrors Nia’s physical movement, making the reader feel as though they are entering the space with her.

Towards the end, the focus narrows to the table and then to Nia’s thoughts and actions. This shift makes the ending feel personal and meaningful, concluding with a sense of quiet anticipation that encourages the reader to continue.
