From the moment Nia reaches the table the tension increases as she becomes more alert after hearing the creak "from somewhere among the shelves." The simple sentence "She froze." causes the reader concern as they wonder what might of happened next and it instantly interests the reader. Despite her heisitance after the sound she continues to stay near the table "though she remained alert." This shows her bravery or perhaps her determination to find out what she needs to find out. We can argue that this pulls the reader to interest as they want to know what she is searching for and why she is in the mysterious library that has been preserved.
