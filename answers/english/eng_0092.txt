The sky felt unsettled. The light dropped to grey; the clouds begin to swirl. A rumbling came up from beneath; the sea began to swell. The sky looked upon them angrily as a clap of thunder filled the evening sky. 

They were sat in their boat: on a trip to nowhere. Silently questioning if this was fate? The rain began to pour already filling the vast sea around them. The boat began to rock, like a nightmarish lullaby putting them to sleep. Just a father and his son on a trip to reconnect, while being oblivious to the blatant signs the universe was sending.

The top of the cliff face caught their eyes. The faded silhouette of an adult and child stood in the distance. But they just stood there, being swallowed by the storm. Nothing would get them to move. 

After son and his dad frantically tried to breach their attention, in a lash of lightning, the man picked up the child and walked down the cliff into the distance. 

Like the rain flooding their boat, their brains were flooded with memories.

Memories back to a time where they were best friends, a father and son relationship that everyone craved, but could they ever go back? Could they ever face a relationship again?

Everything was perfect, a happy family of 3, love shared equally to the fullest of their hearts. Everyday was a barrel of laughs. Everyone loved to play. 

But one day it ended, abruptly; like a dagger to their hearts. Who knew so few words could cause such heartbreak. The family was torn apart. A father and Son feeling like they could never love again. Their relationship flew to shreds, like a tiger clawing at their lives. 

In that moment, they knew they could live with that tiger; they knew they could put it to sleep. They knew that the only way to defeat it was to love again. 

The sky cleared. 

The boat settled. 

Their hearts opened
