The dark,dingy ocean pushed onto the shore,no hesitation to wipe out anything in its path.Clouds above, once calm, now raging with anger poured down onto the sea, making it angrier than ever.The boats were pushed around, flipped over and torn apart by the seas rage. The time pushed closer to midnight,the sea’s frustration not leaving ,getting angrier and angrier by the second. The boats were now nowhere to be seen, the main possibility being murdered by the petrifying sea
