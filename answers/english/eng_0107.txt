"DING DONG" the sound rang out across the dining room, faces perplexed as to who may be accompanying them.

The Baker family had just welcomed in there guest for a joyous meal to celebrate their new business, laughs echoed, drinks clinked, faces stuffed with the endless supply of food that mapped the table everything was normal  for a dinner party. But that was all about to change . 

The doorbell rang again "We aren't expecting anyone else are we" "No I don't think so" his wife replied confused as to who may be at the door. This time Mr Baker got up from his seat a bit hesitant at first brushed off the crumbs that had landed on his clothes from the meal he had just had. The guest still distracting unknowing of what tension had yet to hit the room. The mood had now changed the joyful noise could still be heard from the dining room but a sense of fear hung over Mr Baker. "Who is it" he cried no answer still reluctant he opens the door. There towered over him a ominous figure their face a silhouette and an eerie glow dressed in a cape ripped to the shreds. A man with no possessions but the light he held in his hand. He stood there motionless not one word formed out of his mouth.

Mr Baker still perturbed by the figure at his door peered at him again but this time he looked right into his eyes. "No it can't be..It...can't...BE!" The guest now hearing Mr Bakers cry all rushed to the door the wife at the front before anyone could say another word she dropped to the floor and passed out cold. While the other guest tended to his wife. Transfixed Mr Baker mouthed "Simon? is that you." The once dark ominous figure that had engulfed Simon's body had gone the cape dropped to his ankles. Father.......
