Gusts of wind roared with ferocity, and the clouds above, billowed with pent up fury, mocking its mortal subjects below. Engulfing the sun and swallowing all its warmth, the skies passed out a new doctrine, a totalitarian reign over everything earthly. Keeping the sun as hostage, clouds tore away any happiness and sanctuary, replacing joyous feelings with animosity. Hostile in its nature, the skies rumbled and fought out- crash! A thin streak of white light, so pure and naive to the ways of the world, was barely visible among the uprising of the sky. Rain fell, in a final attempt to force out the unspoken powers of the ocean and entice it out of its peaceful slumber...

With trepidation, and a hefty sigh, the waves which wistfully lulled in soft crests, picked up on the mood-swings in the heavens above, and decided to join the chaos- thrashing, crashing, churning with newfound might, the body of water surged at boats and made quick work of any vessel arrogant enough to face the wrath nature had to offer today. Taunting mercilessly at stragglers. Jeering at lone navy ships. Snarling at the cliffs, daring the rock to break its sturdy shape and join its mindless dance of discord.
