The dark, abandoned doorstep was dimly lit, unaware of the inconspicuous intruder that lurked around it. The light flickered, contributing to the haunted reputation of the villa. Inside, eerie music played at spontaneous intervals, discouraging any child who dared to play ding dong ditch on the accursed place of inhibition.
The intruder approached the manor, unfazed by the rumours. He ascended the creaking stairs that felt like they would cave in any second and send him rolling unto the floor. He approached the door bell and rang it. It seem as if someone approached the door but then,
The frightful melody played.
For 3 consecutive loops, the intruder endured, in the hoops that someone would answer the door. The door knob began to turn.
An old man who appeared visibly distraught by the unexpected visit looked at the intruder, who appeared to have travelled a thousand miles to see him .
"Is this Mr. Reuben's residence" inquired the stranger.
"No" said the old man sharply.
"But"- said the stranger. "No Buts. Now get off my property and be gone" said the old man coldly, slamming the door behind him. 
The stranger turned his back toward the door, almost sceptical about leaving the residence. Turning briefly towards the door, he sighed and began to make his way back home.
If only his father would remember him.
