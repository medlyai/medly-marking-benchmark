The writer describes the orchard at dusks through similes.The orchards trees are described as “Stooped like thoughtful old friends”,suggesting the writer feels a sense of comfort with the trees in the orchards.Another simile, “Like honey dripping from a spoon” implies the fact the the writer loses track of time when they are in the orchard,this may also imply the writer has fun within the orchard.
