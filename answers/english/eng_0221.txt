To the Minister of Education,

I am writing to you, as a young student here in the UK, to desperately ask you to bring about changes in the cruel, life-threatening education system which is putting young people at risk. These innocent, vulnerable people are struggling to keep up with the rigorous pace of the systems you have put in place. You must re-think this for the greater good of all of us and our futures...

Having been a student for the majority of my life, I have struggled immensely at times to cope with the systems that you have established. This problem is vastly worsening by each day, and you have done nothing to alleviate it! Too many times I have not been supported enough, too many times I have seen others' needs dismissed, forgotten. Do you want a country of young people uneducated, unsupported and abandoned in this wormhole of an education? 

Imagine a world where all young people are provided for with all of their educational needs. Grades would be higher, futures would be brighter, and job performance would be at an all time high. In addition, mental health issues which stem from your educational neglect would be massively decreased, improving quality of life. Each student in your education system cannot be generally helped, individual needs must be met if you want a stable and healthy society, which will benefit both you and me. 

Scientists suggest that with additional support provided to students, nearly 99% should be able to feel more confident in their academic ability and perform higher in exams compared to the previous 20% with no support.
