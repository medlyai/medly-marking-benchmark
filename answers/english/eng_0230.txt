Tuesday 30 December 2025

Dear Minister, 

We must revaluate how teenagers - the adults and workforce of tomorrow - are being cared for during some of their more pivotal life stages. If education is being based on old values, we will simply see more and more laziness, underperfomance and a rejection in the education system. One major problem that needs addressing is starting times. It has been repeatedly shown that teenagers' brains do not wake up fully until past 9 oclock, by which time many students have missed out on valuable and integral learning at school. Teachers, parents and students time is being wasted by students being unable to fully concentrate due to tiredness. It also contributes to the growing resentment of the education system - which is highly damaging to everybody concerned. I know personally of many of my peers who have repeated panic attacks or episodes from the thought of coming to school . If that doesnt show a desperate plea for change, what does?
