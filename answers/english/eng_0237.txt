Dear the Minister for Education,

I personally agree with the fact that education needs a radical re-think to meet the needs of students because it has been said by scientists that teenagers are not like adults; they learn and socialise differently and need sleep at different times. 

Pushing teenagers daily, sending them to school to learn and socialise- if they can- to then have to learn, socialise and sleep when they come home. It is physically and emotionally draining them. Their lack of relaxation and sleep is stopping their brains from functioning at their full potential. They cannot concentrate in school.
